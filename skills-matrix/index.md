# Professional Skills Matrix

This section tracks the development of my professional, technical, and academic capabilities throughout the MSc Artificial Intelligence programme.

## Purpose
The Professional Skills Matrix is used to:

- identify skills gained or strengthened during each module
- reflect on personal strengths and development areas
- connect learning outcomes to professional application
- define concrete next steps for continued growth

## Development Areas
This matrix will be updated continuously across the programme, covering areas such as:

- Artificial Intelligence foundations
- Programming and computational thinking
- Data analysis and modelling
- Critical thinking and academic research
- Ethical and responsible AI
- Communication and collaboration
- Leadership and professional practice

## Continuous Improvement
For each module, I will document:

- skills developed
- evidence of development
- practical application
- action plan for further improvement
